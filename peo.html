Program Educational Objectives-1 (PEO-1)

LEADERSHIP

Graduates are Computer Engineers occupying positions of responsibility and leadership requiring highly technical expertise in specialized fields of computer engineering imbibed with ethical values.

Program Educational Objectives-2 (PEO-2)

LIFE-LONG LEARNING

Graduates demonstrate life-long learning and continuous professional and career development while practicing their profession

Program Educational Objectives-3 (PEO-3)

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Graduates will manifest concern for the environment and demonstrate service for the community and society that helps uplift the lives of the people while pursuing entrepreneurial and professional careers.
